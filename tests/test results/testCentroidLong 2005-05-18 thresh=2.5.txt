the equation being minimized is:
   sum(var(r) * numPts(r))
though the final number reported is a normalized version:
   asymm = sum(var(r) * numPts(r)) / (pixNoise^2 * totPts)
where
   pixNoise is the noise/pixel due to read noise and sky
   totPts = sum(numPts(r))

The centroids are randomly distributed over
a range of -FWHM/2, FWHM/2 with respect to the
center of the CCD = the center of the slit.

The slit is along y so the error along y should be smaller than x.
